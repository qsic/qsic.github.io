Goose

Goose is an improv team from NYC. Plus it's a verb. And Goose will do that verb to your mind as they perform their high energy, organic Harold. You can see Goose every Friday at Queen's Secret Improv Club in Long Island City, NY. Also the first Tuesday of every month at the Triple Crown in NYC. Ka-kaw!
