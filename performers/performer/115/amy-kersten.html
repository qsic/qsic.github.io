Amy Kersten

Amy hails from Kansas and started improv as a Williamstown Apprentice. She trained at UCB and is now in the advanced study program. She produces the webseries Hot Mess and also the video blog Coat Tales. https://www.youtube.com/user/HotMesswebseries She is a professional actor and freelance low budget video producer/editor. As a child star she was a featured extra on Unsolved Mysteries.
