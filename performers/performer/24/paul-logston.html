Paul Logston

Paul Logston is an improviser and has been performing since 2005. In 2009 he trained at iO Chicago and returned to the SF Bay Area, co-founding the troupe GranDaddy Charlie. In 2011 he moved to NYC has been studying at the Upright Citizens Brigade and The PIT.
