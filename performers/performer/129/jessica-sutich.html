Jessica Sutich

Jess Sutich is a writer and comedian based in Brooklyn. She recently returned from LA which was a strange time. She writes and performs with groups such as More Than Friends, Baby Yacht and Zealand. She also writes and performs alone as a stand-up and storyteller. Sometimes she sits alone in the house and writes. She has many different degrees of socialization. She has studied at UCB, The Second City theater and Improv Asylum in Boston. She tells stories at places like The Moth, Story Collider, Stripped Stories, and Family Hour with Auntie Sara. She has performed improv and stand-up at The Del Close Marathon, The Women in Comedy Festival, The North Carolina Comedy Arts Festival, and all around NYC, Boston, and LA. She also hosted a storytelling show at improvboston called A Night of Oral (tradition). She was recently named in the top ten of the Final Draft Big Break Contest.
