Megan Doherty

Megan Doherty is a clever comedic actor and sketch comedy and short fiction writer residing in Brooklyn, New York. She has written, directed, and acted in her own sketch comedy shows including Stunted Growth and Information for Children. Information for Children was chosen as The Chicago Sun Times’ Centerstage Critic’s Pick as well as a “must see” by EDGE Chicago magazine. Megan is a graduate of The Second City’s Conservatory Writing Program and was a contributing writer for Navy Pier Pressure in Donny’s Skybox Theatre in Chicago. In New York, Megan performs stand up and is in the advanced improv program at Upright Citizens Brigade. She is in the improv team Florence and the two person long form improvisation team Jo & Meg.
