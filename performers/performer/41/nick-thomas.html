Nick Thomas

Nick Thomas is an actor and performer from Australia who now calls New York City home. He has studied at the People's Improv Theatre, the Magnet Theatre and the U.C.B . He can be seen performing Improv around the city with teams Great White Shark & Goose, and as a member of the sketch troupe Blunderbuss. A qualified chef, he is also quite handy in the kitchen.
