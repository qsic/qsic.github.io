Jon Barcelo

Jon has trained at iO West, Second City LA, as well as both NY and LA UCB sites. He has studied under Amy Seely, Will Hines, Zach Woods, Rebecca Dysdale, Michael Delaney, and Mike Still. He is also a trying sketch writer and storyteller. Jon enjoys performing with his teams Butter High and The Jonoscene, as well as just about anyone else he can get on stage with. He also really enjoys basketball, dumb movies and not falling asleep on the subway...
