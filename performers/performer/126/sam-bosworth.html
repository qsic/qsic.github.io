Sam Bosworth

Sam is a New York City based improviser & drag artist. Sam started performing improv at UMass Amherst with Mission: IMPROVable & has since studied at UCB with Doug Moe, Kate Riley & Patrick Clair. Sam can currently be seen performing with the improv team Zealand.
