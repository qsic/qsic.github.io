Ben Whitehouse

Ben Whitehouse has been studying and performing improv since 2001 and has been obsessed with it ever since. He is the founder and head writer of Improvoker a journal of long-form improvisation. He has studied at the Upright Citizens Brigade Theatre with Shannon O’Neil, Neil Casey, Joe Wengert, Chris Gethard, Anthony King, Billy Merritt, Christina Gausas, Eliza Skinner, Michael Delaney, Rob Riggle, and Matt Walsh. In addition, he has also studied with Armando Diaz of Magnet, Blaine Swen of Improvised Shakespeare, Susan Kaessinger, and Jay Rhoderick of Centralia. Ben is currently in love with coaching improvisation and regularly coaches Dumpster Tequila. Ben coaches improv in New York. Please log in to view coaching/contact info.
