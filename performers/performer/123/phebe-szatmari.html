Phebe Szatmari

Phebe Szatmari is a director, writer, and performer in New York City. She has been studying at the Upright Citizens Brigade since 2008 with Shannon O'Neill, Zach Woods, Ryan Karels, Chelsea Clarke, Fran Gilespie, John Murray, Brandon Scott Jones, Lydia Hensler, Anthony Atamanuik, Shaun Diston, Frank Garcia Hejl, and Damian Chadwick. She found her love for filmmaking while completing her MFA in Creative Writing and Literature at Stony Brook Southampton. Phebe has won scholarships and awards for her filmmaking and writing, namely the 20/20/20 Digital Filmmaking Scholarship given by Christine Vachon of Killer Films in association with Stony Brook Southampton's Digital Filmmaking program. Phebe is currently directing/producing four web series, a feature-length comedy, two pilots for TV, and several shorts. She produces and is a director of photography on the digital filmmaking team, UCB1, at the Upright Citizens Brigade. When she's not making comedy videos, she's performing improv all over NYC. She's on a house team, TALON, at the Queens' Secret Improv Club, and her independent improv groups include Mourning Wood, HUNS, Plague, and Witch. You can also find her singing at open mics as the PB of PB&J.;
