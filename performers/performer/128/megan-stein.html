Megan Stein

Like at least several others on this website, Megan Stein is an improvisor living in New York City. She has studied at UCB under Keisha Zollar, Kate Riley, Anthony Atamanuik, and Kevin Hines. She also does things like act, direct, and write. She also produces films with her company PrashNYC. She also hosts a monthly comedy show called Sunday School with fellow female person Kelsey McLaughlin. She also makes a mean coffee cake.
