Rhonda Underwood

Rhonda is from the land of palm trees and serial killers, a.k.a. Florida. She has studied improv at both The PIT and The Magnet. She put her skills to use performing with Assembly Line and Butter High. Come check her out with Butter High on Friday nights at the QSIC in LIC! In Rhonda's spare time, she works at Revlon to make the world a prettier place. She enjoys empanadas, Hello Kitty, pimento cheese, sweet tea, beer and college football. GO FSU! If you really want to get to know Rhonda, ask her about her 14 years of experience in the competitive world of beauty pageants and why Xavier Roberts is a name she'll never forget.
