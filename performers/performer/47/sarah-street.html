Sarah Street

Sarah originally hails from Cork City in Ireland. Having studied acting,music and dance from a young age,she decided to move to the big apple to pursue her dream. She studied at the NY Conservatory of Dramatic Arts. Since graduating she has performed in numerous show,done a commercial for NBC and has just completed her first role in a feature film. She has just recently discovered her love for musical improv and joined the wonderful "Petting Zoo" and loves performing with them around the city.She has studied and continues to study at both UCB and Magnet theatre.To keep up to date with Sarah you can visit her at http://therealsarahstreet.tumblr.com/ or tweet her at @sarahstreet7
