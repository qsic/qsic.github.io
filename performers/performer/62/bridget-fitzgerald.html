Bridget Fitzgerald

BRIDGET FITZGERALD is an actress & comedian who's pleased as punch to meet you. FEATURE FILMS: Joe (supporting role opposite Armand Assante), Things I Don’t Understand (Best Feature, Indie Sprit Film Fest), Pearl, Life Is A Dream, Marked Man SHORT FILMS: Wedding Day, Those Kids In Brooklyn. SITCOM PILOTS: Musie, Connie, Hot Date (NYTVF Microsoft/Bing Finalist, 2011). WRITER & PRODUCER: Calling On Angels, Bridget Fitzgerald: Dance Machine, Killing Senses. UCB STREET CREDZZZ: The Time Capsule, Planet Of People, UCBW as the Lunchlady, Too Many Guns, Mrs. Darcy, Vantage Point, The Convergence, Cool Show 4 Teen Girls, Acts of Distraction: A Superhero Sketch Show. Bridget's done improv in NY since 2006 & standup since 2009. Proud card-carrying member of SAG-AFTRA & the Tasti-D Fan Club. For upcoming films & other information, check out Bridget on IMDb, on twitter as @smileybridge, or on her homepage for further information. Thanks for stopping in, and hope you have a delightful day!
