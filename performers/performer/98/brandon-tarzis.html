Brandon Tarzis

Brandon grew up in Columbus, Ohio, where he felt like he needed to leave the whole time. He went to college and wasted his time learning television and radio. Now, he's in New York and has studied at the UCB under Silvija Ozols, Mike Still, Ari Voukydis, Gavin Speiller, Will Hines, Kevin Hines, Abra Tabak and has taken workshops with John Frusciante, Shannon O'Neill, Ben Schwartz, Michael Delaney, Brian Faas, and Rob Riggle. He's super stoked to be in a place he likes, doing what he likes.
