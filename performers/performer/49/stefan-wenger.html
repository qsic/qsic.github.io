Stefan Wenger

Stefan Wenger has been telling tall tales most of his life, and now he gets to do it on stage. With a background in performance poetry and singing, Stefan's been trained by the Magnet and is all about the musical improv nowadays, performing with Petting Zoo every week, and also co-hosting the Magnet Musical Mixer with the rest of Snake Eyes. He's a native New Yorker, his ethnic background is Amish/Mennonite and he's into yoga and energetic healing work and other stuff that's weirder still.
