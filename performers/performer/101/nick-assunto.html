Nick Assunto

Nick moved to New York in May of 2011 to study improv at the Upright Citizen's Brigade Theater. He has learned from such legends as Zach Woods, Erik Tanoyue, Gavin Speiller, Porter Mason, Doug Moe, Chelsea Clarke, Abra Tabak, Josh Patten, John Timothy and Nate Dern. Outside of UCB he has learned from David Razowsky, Chris Aurilio, Jen Curran, Ali Fahranakian and Greg Desantis. Past Improv Teams Include: Alabasterd, Ghetto Bird, Marshall and the Intern, (Saxophone Music), Fetch, and Smooch Patrol. Now he lives in LA! He moved there September of 2013.
