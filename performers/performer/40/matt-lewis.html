Matt Lewis

Matt stumbled upon improv in, of all places, Greensboro, NC, where he studied "short form" with Steve Lesser and Jennie Stencel at the Idiot Box. Once he got a taste for that sweet drug improvisation, there was no stopping him. He moved to NYC and has been studying at the UCB with Chelsea Clarke, Ryan Karels, Doug Moe, and Brandon Gardner. He has been known to compare the need for improv to that of a crack binge. Don't get in his way if he needs a fix.
