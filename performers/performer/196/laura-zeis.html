Laura Zeis

Laura Zeis is originally from Duncanville, Texas and has BA in Theatre Arts from Hendrix College in Arkansas. She is an actor, comedian, dancer and artist living in Brooklyn, NY. Laura has done improv since 2006 and stand-up since 2011. She can be found on twitter as @zeistea. She loves baking, photography, and is a member of the Museum of Natural History.
