Kelsey McLaughlin

Kelsey McLaughlin is an improvisor, actor, and writer in NYC. She started improvising in Boston, performing in ImprovBoston's "Face Off," improvised play-making group, "First Impact," and her school troupe, "NU & Improv'd." Kelsey is studying at UCB and The Magnet. If you hear Kelsey tell you she can freestyle, she is blacked out and should really go home.
