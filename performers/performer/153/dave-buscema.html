Dave Buscema

Dave Buscema performs every Wednesday night with circuit team Face Card at The Queen's Secret Improv Club. He loves playing with this latest incarnation of the team formerly known as 50% Dave and doesn't miss having his name as part of the team's title since the faulty math always nagged him. Plus, it puts Dave's number of teams he's been involved with that include the word face at two, a new point of pride. Dave, who has studied at UCB and the Magnet, was a founding member of Baby FACE, a group inspired and coached by the pioneering jazz/improv team, FACE, using jazz music to influence scenes. Dave has also enjoyed playing with indie team The Elusive Rock, a spawn of the extremely enjoyable Improv in the Park, a meeting of improvisers of all age and experience levels in parks and indoor public spaces across New York City.
