Meghan Ross

Meghan has studied sketch writing and improv at Upright Citizens Brigade Theatre in New York since October 2011. She is available to play your ethnically obscure best friend at any of your social events. She performs with indie teams Jacob's Parting Gift and A Few Good Dudes.
