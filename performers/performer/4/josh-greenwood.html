Josh Greenwood

Josh is an actor and writer based in Williamsburg Brooklyn. He likes to do improv, sketch comedy, theatre, and make films; sometimes at the same time. Josh is also a sponsored professional Slackline athlete on the US Men’s Pro Team for Gibbon Slacklines. He is currently ranked 12th in the world for competitive Slacklining. Being from Colorado, Josh likes to go outside…a lot. Find out more about Josh at http://josh-greenwood.com/
