Jewel Elizabeth

Jewel Simone Elizabeth pretends to be people for money. When it comes to improv, she will pretend to be animals for free. She's a UCB-trained Improviser, Actress and Host. She currently performs on the house team ZEALAND at QSIC every Friday at 7. Currently performing: Fridays with ZEALAND Supernova Sundays with TEAM RACHEL Thursdays on e-mprov.com with HECTOR Sporadically with UCB indie team HAPPY DINOSAUR You can find her interview show for Backstage Magazine at www.JewelElizabeth.com.
