Lauren Guenther

Lauren has been improvising in New York City since 2002. She has performed with Instant Cinema (UCB, original cast), King Tiny (PIT house team), Maude, Boldfaced Liar (PIT house team) the improv tragedy group HARSH, The End of the World, and UCB Harold Team Scootch. Lauren currently performs with the Queens Secret Improv Club house team Sister Sex Wolf. She has studied with Amy Poehler, Ian Roberts, Armando Diaz, Paul Scheer, Ari Voukydis, Anthony King, Will Hines, and Joe Bill among others. Lauren coaches improv in New York.
