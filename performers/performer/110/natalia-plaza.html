Natalia Plaza

Natalia is apart of the improv graduate program at the UCBT. She has been a stage actor since childhood and has played the lead in many film projects. Now she is concentrating on her comedy and acting career full time and quit her part time job as a registered dental hygienist bc her dentist boss sucked ass. When she isn't practicing she is writing, performing and viewing improv or asking people if they like butts. She enjoys baby cows and talking about the health properties of garlic.
