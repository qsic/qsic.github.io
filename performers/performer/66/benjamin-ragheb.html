Benjamin Ragheb

Benjamin Ragheb has been improvising since February 2007. He has studied at the Upright Citizens Brigade Theatre under Betsy Stover, Neil Casey, Will Hines, Joe Wengert, Will Hines, Anthony King, Will Hines, and Shannon O'Neill. He is a member of Fat Penguin, the indie team that almost beat Derrick in Cage Match that one time. He is also a member of Zuleyka. Apart from the above, he has no notable accomplishments. Benjamin coaches improv in New York. Please log in to view coaching/contact info.
