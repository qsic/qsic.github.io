Carrie Tavris

Carrie Tavris made her career starring in numerous un-finshed short films, non-union commercials and secret theatrical projects. She is a proud member of Dream Date and the musical improv group, Meaningful Touch. The second round of The Magnet Circuit will feature her brand-new, sparkly, yet-to-be-named Harold Team. When not trying to be funny, Carrie spends her time loving up Brooklyn, drinking smoothies, and playing with her yoga monkey.
