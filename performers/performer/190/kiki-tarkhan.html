Kiki Tarkhan

Kiki Tarkhan is a writer and performer living in Brooklyn. She has studied improv at UCB since 2012, and you can find her doing shows in basements and bars all over New York City. She holds a master's from Emerson and a bachelor's from Providence College. She loves jokes.
