Matt Morea

Matt has studied at improv at the Magnet Theater under the auspices of Rick Andrews, Russ Armstrong, and Peter McNerney. He's also studied sketch writing at UCB NY and is a former member of the sketch group Uncle Space Ranch. Matt does voice overs for corporate videos and commercials. His website can be seen here.
